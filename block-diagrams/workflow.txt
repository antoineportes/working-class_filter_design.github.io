1 . Create TikZ diagram on Overleaf.
2 . Download **pdf** and text files (to corresponding directory: ./block-diagrams/chapterXX/ ).
3a. Open pdf with Inkscape:
   --> clip to media box
   --> draw missing fonts
   --> DO NOT embed images
   --> DO NOT import pages
3b. If 3a poops in the soup:
   --> Cairo import
   --> Select all
   --> Path > Objects to Path
   --> Ungroup/Flatten -- repeat.
4 . Group layers as: FILL (basically text), BOTH (basically arrows heads) & STROKE (basically everything else).
5 . Add CSS classes: .has-fill & .has-stroke accordingly.
6 . Save as **plain SVG**.
7 . CTRL+F: replace all: id=" with: id="CC-SS-DD-    //  meaning: chapter-section-diagram w/ zero-padding.
8 . Add to HTML in:
   div.diagram-container > div.block-diagram#CC-SS-DD{}
